General

ZIM Connections is a UK-based company that provides affordable mobile plans to connect travellers everywhere at all times.

We partner with mobile providers worldwide to offer travellers a solution that allows them to buy an eSIM plan from a local provider before departure and activate it upon arrival. eSIM technology is virtual; no need for physical SIM cards to get sent to customers. Thus, offering a convenient, time-saving, and low-cost solution.
